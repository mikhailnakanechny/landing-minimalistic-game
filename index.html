Description

Battle begins, have player and monster on 100hp or more.

The player chooses the spell first.

The player goes to the task screen.

In the case of a successful decision - the spell is used, otherwise the transition speed towards the monster. Monster uses a spell, deals damage to the player.

If the player won the battle with the next monster.

If you lose - shows a table of records
